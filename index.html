About

Hi! I'm Jacky G. Ratio, you can just call me Jack. I'm a first-year student at Ateneo de Naga University pursuing a BS in Digital Illustration and Animation.

Growing up, I dreamed of becoming an architect or engineer, inspired by watching my father create. But a friendship with a talented artist introduced me to digital art, and I realized it was my true passion. So, when I learned about ADNU SHS's AD-2D Animation and Visual Effects (AVFX) strand, I knew it was perfect for me. AVFX taught me the fundamentals of art, animation, storytelling, and digital tools like Photoshop, and visual effects, helping me grow both artistically and personally. As an editorial cartoonist for ADNU SHS’s publication, I also discovered that art could be a powerful voice for raising awareness on important issues.

Now, I'm at the start of my college journey, studying the course I love, with many artworks and challenges ahead. To keep myself motivated, I compiled my digital artworks, from my very first piece to my latest, a reminder of how far I've come. This collection reminds me to be confident even when I doubt myself, and I hope it might inspire others too!
